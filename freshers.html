Find a church

We all love CU, but it's no substitute for a great church.

Here at LSESUCU, our main purpose is to know Christ and make Him known, but getting stuck into a church is important for growth in our personal relationship with God with the support of a diverse church family.

In London, we are spoilt for choice many great local choices from all denominations, small and large. CU members attend a variety of churches, some of which are linked below.

We'll host a church-finding picnic, and CU members would love to take you and introduce you to the churches they attend.

Join us on Sunday 5th October at 1pm at Lincoln's Inn Fields.

Alongside Sunday services, many of these churches also have mid-week small-group bible studies for students - a great way to get to know like-minded Christians in London.
