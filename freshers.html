There are plenty more events still to be announced, so watch this space!

Find a church

We all love CU, but it is no substitute for a great church.

Here at LSESUCU, our main purpose to know Christ and make Him known, but getting stuck into a church is important for growth in our personal relationship with God with the suppose of a diverse church family.

In London, we are spoilt for choice many great local choices from all denominations, small and large. CU members attend a variety of these, some of which are linked below.

During the first month we will host a church-finding picnic and CU members would love to take you and introduce you to the churches they attend.

Along side Sunday services, many of these churches also have mid-week small-group bible studies for students - a great way to get to know like-minded Christians in London.
